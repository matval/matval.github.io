    I'm Mateus Valverde Gasparino

    Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science

    Hello! I'm Mateus, a roboticist working in the robotics navigation for unstructured environments.

    • Lives In: Illinois, US
    • University: University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign
    • I'm a full stack roboticist! I've worked on robot design, low level control, and sensor fusion. Currently, I work with learning-based perception, traversability prediction and learning-based control.
